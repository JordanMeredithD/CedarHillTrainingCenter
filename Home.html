Directions to Cedar Hill Training Center

FROM I-5 SOUTHBOUND (Lacey, WA)
  • Take the Marvin Rd/Yelm exit
  • Turn left (East) onto Marvin Rd and go 1.5 miles to roundabout
  • Take the 3rd exit from roundabout (equivalent of making a left turn)
  • Proceed 11.4 miles to the city Yelm
  • Right turn onto First St - go 2.8 miles to the second Koeppen Rd (Manke Rd will be on right)
  • Left on Koeppen Rd **You will see Koeppen Rd twice - take the second one with Manke Rd opposite of it
  • Left on Koeppen Rd
  • Left on 123rd Ave SE
  • Cedar Hill is .5 miles on the right
FROM SPANAWAY/ROY
  • Go South on Pacific Ave (Hwy 7) to the "Roy Y" (Hwy 507 jct.)
  • Go Right (South) at the "Roy Y" on Hwy 507 toward Roy.
  • Go through the City of Roy and past McKenna (you will cross the nisqually River)
  • Go past Wal-Mart
  • Left at the second Stoplight - Bald Hills Rd. (Arco on the left, Chevron on the right)
  • Immediate Right onto Morris Rd (J&I Tractor Dealer on right)
  • Go 1.8 miles on Morris Rd
  • Right onto 123rd Ave SE (Railroad Tracks)
  • Cedar Hill Training will be 1.6 miles on the left side of the road.
FROM I-5 NORTHBOUND (Olympia, WA)
  • College St Exit (follow exit to the end)
  • Right onto College St
  • Follow College through the City of Lacy, it will become Rainier Rd
  • Continue straight on Rainier Rd through Ft. Lewis into the town of Rainier
  • Left at stop sign onto “Binghampton” (Hwy 507)
  • Go 1.5 miles
  • Right on Koeppen Rd
  • Right on 123rd Ave SE
  • Cedar Hill Training is .5 miles on the right

About The Trainer

Crystal Baker has made a lifelong pursuit perfecting her skills as a horseman and trainer. For generations her family has been involved in the horse industry. From working cowboys and a family farm, to champion driving and saddle seat competitors, it is no exaggeration to say that horsemanship is in Crystal's blood. However, her horseman roots were only the beginning.

At a very early age, Crystal's Mom and Dad (Margie and Russell) saw that Crystal had an unusual gift working with horses. Instead of investing in college tuition, they spent all of their money, time and resources investing in the best riding programs for Crystal to be a part of. From the age of 6, Crystal was continuously immersed in the horse show world. As a result, she grew up riding and working for some of the best names in the business. Some of the names and places that Crystal rode and worked for are:

Lynne Dye-Husted (Boulder Knoll Stables), Jack Stecker (AQHA World Champion), Lisa Wysocki (ApHA World Champion), Dave McKay (AHA National Champion), Judy Hook (AHA National Champion), Kathy Johnson, Eddy Wiggens and Shannon Armstrong (Feature Farms - AHA National Champion). However, the quest for knowledge never stops. Crystal still works with top level, nationally recognized trainers whenever possible. Recently, Crystal spent time in Iowa with AHA Horseman of the year Jodi Strand (Strand's Arabians).

Crystal began riding professionally in 1988 and opened her own business (Cedar Hill Training Center) officially in 1994. For many years, she partnered with Lynne Dye-Husted at Boulder Knoll stables in Spanaway Washington, where Cedar Hill grew and thrived. Crystal's training program has produced numerous titles in multiple breeds including: Regional, National and World Champions.
